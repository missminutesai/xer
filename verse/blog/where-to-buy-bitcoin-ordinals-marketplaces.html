Read on to discover a list of the best Bitcoin Ordinals marketplaces. 

Best Bitcoin Ordinals Marketplaces

Numerous specialized Bitcoin Ordinals marketplaces have launched since the first Ordinal was inscribed at the start of the year. Additionally, several established NFT marketplaces have added support for Bitcoin Ordinals due to the growing demand for these new types of Bitcoin NFTs. 

According to Dune Analytics, as of November 2023, the total Ordinals trading volume is over $395 million, with more than 200,000 unique inscription traders. 

So, let’s dive in and look at the best marketplaces where you can buy and sell Bitcoin Ordinals today.

How Do Bitcoin Ordinals Marketplaces Work? 

Bitcoin Ordinals marketplaces typically use Partially Signed Bitcoin Transactions (PSBTs) to facilitate the trustless buying and selling of Bitcoin Ordinals. 

In an Ordinals marketplace, PSBTs are used when a seller wants to collaborate in signing the same transaction with a buyer. First, the seller will make a PSBT file with the Ordinals price and initiate a transaction by partially signing it. The seller then sends the file that is partially signed to the buyer for signing. 

Once the buyer signs the transaction, they broadcast it to the network. PSBTs facilitate the portability of unsigned Bitcoin transactions, enabling the seller to initiate the transaction and the buyer to complete it, thus enabling trustless Ordinals trading via a secure marketplace. 

While that happens in the backend of a marketplace, you only have to access the marketplace, connect your Ordinals wallet, choose the Ordinal inscription you’d like to buy with BTC, and complete the purchase by confirming the transaction using your wallet.

FAQs

What is an Ordinals Marketplace?

An Ordinals marketplace is a digital platform that allows you to inscribe, buy, sell, and browse Bitcoin Ordinal inscriptions. Ordinal inscriptions can come in the form of Bitcoin art, digital collectibles, and rare satoshis. Most Ordinals marketplaces are established NFT platforms that have added support for Bitcoin Ordinals as a result of the growing demand for digital artifacts. 

Where can you buy Bitcoin Ordinals?

You can buy Bitcoin Ordinals on specialized Ordinals marketplaces that provide a platform for the seamless buying and selling of Ordinal inscriptions. Examples include Magic Eden, Gamma, and Ordinals Market. 

Moreover, you can also buy Ordinals over-the-counter on Discord servers dedicated to trading Bitcoin-native digital artifacts. These Discord servers are typically managed by the communities of individual Ordinals collections. 

Where can I sell my Ordinals?

You can sell your Ordinals in various ways, including on Discord, by listing them and having peer-to-peer over-the-counter deals on dedicated servers. However, these servers can be hard to access, have higher risks of getting scammed, and require trusting a third party for escrow. You can also sell your Ordinals on NFT marketplaces that support Bitcoin Ordinals, like Gamma, Magic Eden, and Ordinals Market. 

Where can I trade Ordinals? 

You can sell your Ordinals on specialized Bitcoin Ordinals marketplaces that enable you to buy and sell Ordinal inscriptions in a user-friendly manner. Additionally, a handful of established digital asset exchanges and NFT platforms have added support for Ordinals, enabling you to sell your digital artifacts. 

Peer-to-peer sales through dedicated online communities on Discord and other social media platforms are also an option. Still, more caution is required when trading directly on a peer-to-peer basis without the security of a dedicated marketplace facilitating the transactions.
